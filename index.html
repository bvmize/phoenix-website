About Us 🔥

Founded in 2010, Raleigh Phoenix is the premier team representing North Carolina in USAU's Club Women's Division. Phoenix prides itself on developing homegrown talent from the Triangle and beyond, including representatives of UNC, NC State, Wake Forest, UNC Wilmington, Davidson, and other regional schools. On the field, Phoenix has a long history of success, including two semifinal appearances in 2011 and 2021, nine nationals appearances, 🥉 at WUCC in 2022, and many sectional and regional 🥇. Phoenixers have been recognized for their talent on the field as well as for their contributions off it, coaching youth and college teams to greatness (see: UNC, Triangle YCC teams), serving as leaders and organizers within the community, and being key pieces of other, far-flung teams and ultimate organizations.

As for team philosophy, the founders of Phoenix put it this way in 2010:

"The Phoenix is a vibrant, mythical bird that will typically live 500 to 1000 years and then build a nest. The Phoenix will ignite the nest and burn fiercely until out of the embers and ashes a young Phoenix is reborn anew. Phoenix recognizes that a tradition of winning has been built by the sweat of previous generations. We respect and thank the players who have gone before us. It is with our deepest gratitude that we go forward, hoping that we may ignite a history of excellence as long as the previous."
